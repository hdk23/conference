About the Developer

Henry Kim is a student web programmer from Seoul, South Korea attending Dartmouth College ('23). After enjoying his first programming class during his freshman year of high school, he has pursued a major in computer science and seeks to broaden his interests into interdisciplinary fields with a computing component. After taking AP Computer Science A in his junior year and Harvard Summer School's "Web Programming with Python and Javascript" course, he has come to appreciate the power and art of programming and algorithmic design thinking. During his senior year of high school, he has designed a "matchmaking site" for a Student Council fundraiser to encourage more students to attend prom, matching students based on their responses to a questionnaire by reporting a ranking of students based on their match scores. Through his four years at Dartmouth, he seeks to delve deeper into upper-level computer science to solve more challenging and meaningful problems. If you have any questions about the website or would like to contact the web developer for any reason, please feel free to email hkim.23@dartmouth.edu for any inquiries.
